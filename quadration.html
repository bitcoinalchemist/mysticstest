Planets

Every card in a spread sits beneath a planetary influence, read left to right from Neptune through to Mercury. The planet colours how that card expresses itself across a given area of life.

Info

Every playing card corresponds to a day of the year, turning the familiar 52-card deck into a map of time and personality. Each person carries a single Birth Card, set by their date of birth.

Your Birth Card

Your Birth Card comes from your birthday through the Solar Value: sv = 55 − (2 × month + day). That number points to one of the 52 cards. December 31 is the exception, belonging to the Joker, which stands outside the system. Look yours up with the Finder.

Spreads and Quadrations

The deck can be laid out in a grid of seven planetary rows, crowned by three master cards. Reshuffling that layout by a fixed rule called a Quadration moves every card to a new seat. Ninety Quadrations bring the deck back to where it began, so each age from 0 to 89 has its own spread. The age selector steps through them, showing how your influences shift across a lifetime.

The Life Script

Reading across the seven planetary columns, from Mercury through to Neptune, gives your Life Script: the cards that govern your mind, love, drive, fortune, lessons, freedom and dreams. See Planets for what each one represents.

Relationships

Add a second birthday in the Finder to see how two people connect. Their Solar Values are combined to reveal the card that describes the bond between them.
